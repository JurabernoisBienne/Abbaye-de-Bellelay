1890-2022: Canton ownership and clinical usage shaped many buildings and layouts.

1797-1890: Industrial and manufacturing uses after desacralization during the French period.

1714-1797: Baroque rebuilding and establishment of a training boarding school.

1136-1714: Foundation as a monastery with pilgrim reception and local production activities.
